Espresso Martini Cake Recipe
For when you're a coffee AND cake addict...

Ingredients:

  • 285g soft unsalted butter
  • 165g golden caster sugar
  • 100g soft light brown sugar
  • 4 medium eggs, beaten
  • 200g self-raising flour
  • 25g cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tbsp espresso powder
  • 50ml coffee liqueur

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oven to 180°C, fan 160°C, gas 4. Grease and line two 20cm round sandwich tins.
  2. Beat the butter and both sugars together until very light and creamy. Add the eggs a little at a time, beating between each addition. Sift over the flour, a pinch of salt, the cocoa and baking powder and fold into the wet mixture until just combined. Pour in the cooled espresso and coffee liqueur and gently mix.
  3. Divide the mixture equally between the prepared tins and level out. Bake in the oven for 25-30 minutes, until the cakes spring back to the touch and a skewer inserted into the middle comes out clean.
  4. Leave the cakes to cool in the tins for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and allow to cool completely.
